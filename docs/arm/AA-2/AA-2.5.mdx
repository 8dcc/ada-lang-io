<p>[A <code><a href="../AA-2/AA-2.5#S0015">character_literal</a></code> is formed by enclosing a graphic character between two apostrophe characters.] <br /></p>


#### Syntax

<p><code>character_literal</code><a id="S0015"></a><code> ::= </code>'<code>graphic_character</code>'<br /></p>

<Admonition type="aarm" aarm="note" title="Note: ">
NOTE 1   A <code><a href="../AA-2/AA-2.5#S0015">character_literal</a></code> is an enumeration literal of a character type. See 3.5.2. <br /></Admonition>


#### Examples

<p><em>Examples of character literals:</em> <br /></p>

<CodeBlock>
--  {"{"}{"AI95-00433-01"}{"}"}{"\n"} 'A'     '*'     '''     ' '{"\n"}'L'     ''     ''    -- Various els.{"\n"}''     ''            -- Big numbers - infinity and aleph.{"\n"}

</CodeBlock>
